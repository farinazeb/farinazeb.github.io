Offerings

Sound Bath Meditation

Immersive sound experiences designed to support rest, ease, and nervous-system downshifting. Participants relax comfortably while listening to layered, soothing tones.

Creative Arts Workshops

Guided workshops using visual art, movement, writing, and mixed media as practices of presence, expression, and calm. No artistic background required.

Private & Group Experiences

Custom sessions for individuals, small groups, and organizations—ideal for team wellness, community events, or intimate gatherings.

What to Expect

Come as you are. Sessions are designed to be gentle and accessible. You’ll be invited to slow down, explore sound and creativity, and leave feeling more grounded and refreshed.

  • Low-pressure environment focused on exploration (not performance)
  • All experience levels welcome
  • Supportive structure for relaxation and creative expression

About En’Chant Studio

Based in Central Virginia, En’Chant Studio blends sound and creative practices to support everyday well-being. Offerings are designed for personal enrichment, relaxation, and expressive exploration.
